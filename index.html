About Me

Tatu Putto, Full-Stack Web Developer

or atleast aspiring to be one someday

Hi there! My name is Tatu Putto, I am a 24 years old web developer from Joensuu, Finland. I graduated in March, 2017 from Karelia University of Applied Sciences with a bachelor's degree in Business Information Technology. I do all sorts of web development projects with a number of different programming languages and technologies. I mainly use JavaScript, HTML, CSS, Node, Java, MongoDB and MySQL in my projects. I also have some exprerience in UI and UX design, but I much rather focus on the implementation than design.

My focus started shifting to front-end development during the latter part of my studies due to my intership at Sportspot and more recently my thesis projects practical part. While both of those projects included alot of back-end work aswell My skillset leans towards front-end development
